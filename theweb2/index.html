Your health, Your Therapy

We are passionate about using technology to improve the quality of life of its users, and as athletes, we know how critical physical therapy is to a better quality of life. Physical therapy and healthcare should be all about the patients, but right now physical therapists have no way of knowing whether patients have completed their exercises, whether it's been completed correctly, and whether patients are in pain. And that's why we've put aside our basketball rivalry to make physical therapy finally all about YOU.
